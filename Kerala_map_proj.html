Kerala, located in the southern part of India, is a beautiful coastal state, with a rich cultural heritage and diverse geography, worth to celebrate. It is divided into 14 districts, each with its unique charm and vibe, from the tranquil backwaters of Alappuzha to the misty hills of Wayanad. Ernakulam is the commercial hub, while Thiruvananthapuram is the state capital, Kozhikode is known for its historical significance, and Kannur for its beaches to be at. No matter which district you visit, you'll be greeted with warmth and hospitality, making Kerala a must-visit destination for anyone seeking natural beauty and cultural diversity
